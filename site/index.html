AC Transit performance

System summary

On time = arrived at the scheduled time, up to 3 min late (0 ≤ delay ≤ 3 min). Early = before scheduled time (delay < 0). Late = more than 3 min after schedule (delay > 3 min).

Cumulative on-time at looser thresholds

Same numerator (arrived at-or-after schedule), but counting anyone who arrived within the larger window. Within 5 min = 0 ≤ delay ≤ 5 min. Within 7 min = 0 ≤ delay ≤ 7 min.

Delay distribution (1-min buckets)

Each bar = one whole-minute bucket of the schedule-vs-actual delay, counted across all observed stop arrivals. Outliers beyond −15 min or +45 min are clamped to the boundary buckets.

Schedule compliance

Service delivered = percent of trips scheduled today that we observed running (≥1 stop arrival recorded). 99%+ is good, under 90% is concerning. Dropped trips are scheduled trips with no observed arrivals.

    Headway distortion

    For each observed stop arrival, we compare the bus's lateness/earliness to the scheduled gap to its neighboring scheduled trip at the same stop on the same route. Late buses use the prior scheduled trip's gap; early buses use the next.

    Example. A 10-min headway with a bus 6 min late = a +60% distortion (the gap from the previous bus stretched from 10 to 16 min). A 5-min-early arrival on a 5-min headway is a −100% distortion — the bus collapsed onto the previous scheduled slot. Higher absolute values = more disruptive to riders waiting at the stop.
